Qantas Insurance and Flight Booking

Travelling in 2022 and beyond

The great travel reset of 2022 is the biggest challenge in Qantas’s 102 year history

Background

COVID-19 was the single largest event in my life and all of a sudden, everyone’s lives froze and as a result, we came out the other side with new found behaviours that would forever shape how we approached all the things we thought were simple - like going overseas for leisure, adventure, exploration and business.

As the pandemic started to become manageable, I took my first trip overseas to Singapore in December of 2021 and the freedom of being able to plan a spontaneous trip became a whole new world of doubts, what ifs and paperwork.

While I was overseas I was taking notes about my experience which would shape some of the early concepts I wanted to present back and to subsequently conduct wider qualitative research with Australians from NSW and Victoria.

Discovery: contextual analysis and interviews

We conducted interviews and concept testing with customers that are expecting to travel in the next 12 months or have travelled in the past 12 months. We excluded all customers that get complimentary travel insurance as part of their credit card.
