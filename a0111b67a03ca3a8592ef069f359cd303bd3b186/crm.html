CommBank CRM Digital Transformation (2016 - 2018)

CommSee is one of the biggest in-house CRM system ever built by a bank and is strictly proprietary internal tools, therefore I am unable to show you what I worked on. However, I can tell you this product is used in the bank everyday by tens of thousands of CBA staff to search, transact, product originations, financial record-keeping, fraud detection, branch functions, loan decisioning and analytics for retail, business and institutional customers. Most of my time was spent consulting with these teams.

Problem space

Hundreds of applications developed internally were never designed for usability and were built and modified with little regards for consistency

Challenges:
  • Simple transactions grew in complexity due to a mix of old legacy systems and new web applications
  • Training staff became costly and process complexities began to emerge that impacted staff productivity
  • Applications were not used by a single team but shared and used across the group (many stakeholders with different goals)
Strategy:
  • Using web technologies to build internals for retail, business and institutional staff
  • Enterprise Design System was developed, applied and extended by designers working with project scrums
  • Service Design Framework used to better understand the interactions between the bank, our staff and customers and the challenges of implementing omni-channel experiences
  • Frequent observation and testing with CBA staff members on all major projects
I was involved across business units on projects with:
  • Financial crimes and regulatory - Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA) and Common Reporting Standard (CRS)
  • Home Loans - Dynamic Decisioning for Home Loans
  • Credit Cards - Product Origination and AMEX
  • Comprehensive Credit Reporting (CCR) - Interbank Credit Reporting
  • Settlements for Institutional Banking - Settling of High Value Loan Facilities
