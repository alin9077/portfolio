PrettyGreen Mobile App

By the end of 2019, PrettyGreen.com.au had proved a sustainable business that delivers Australian made consumable products and gift baskets directly to corporate customers across Australia. In 2020, we are building a mobile web app on Shopify's infrastruture to deliver a range of boutique small batch produce and products to Sydney metro areas by Christmas 2020.

Project constraints

1 month to produce a MVP to test in North Shore local area to test and learn, determine market fit before continuing progressive national rollout.

Working directly with the Managing Director. My responsibility solely focused on UI and interaction design. The first release of this project only had the luxury of a single month for delivery which meant my approach was geared towards a delivery and timeline focused design process.

Selective tastebuds and diets

Apart from capturing login, personal and delivery details. We encourage users to optionally provide their household details like pets and any particular dietary requirements like vegetarian/vegan/gluten-free.

These details help us provide a personalised product experience. Knowing whether the user is vegetarian allows us to filter out all meat products and use that space for more suitable products.

Product preview

We devised three levels of interaction, each with an overarching purpose to keep the product feed simple and uncluttered when users are browsing and products are competing for users attention

1st level contain only the most basic information like photo, title and price.
2nd level will give additional details like a description, seasonality and an ‘add to cart’ button.
3rd level contains the rest of the information and related products.

Product page

The 'Add to cart' button default state is displayed to provide complete clarity on the single most important action on the product page and tie the page around this action. We then identified 3 types of products relevant to this component. 1. A single item like a bottle of wine. 2. Multiple items like 8 apples. 3. Large quantity of items for bulk purchase. This component was picked for it's flexibility and scalability.
