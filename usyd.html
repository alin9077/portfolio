University of Sydney
Architecture Faculty

Going to University is a life-changing experience. For many senior high school students and young adults interested in studying architecture, they're going through handbooks, multiple web services and social media to find information for things like entry requirements, units of study, architecture styles and student experiences. This was troublesome and uninspiring for future students. For a design faculty, the pages were never designed.

Problem space

Information about architecture courses offered in the faculty were scattered across independent channels. There was no central oversight and an unclear digital strategy

Additionally, our sites didn't scale for mobile and had accessibility issues for the vision impared.

Entry requirements, unit of study & elective information, handbooks, PDFs and social media channels

Course progression and details

Students often find themselves tracking back and forth between the unit of study search and the faculty pages that contained course progression details

We digitised and pulled all course unit information into a centralised location where users could explore what 3 years studying architecture looked like.

Electives

A part of any University experience is the idea of broadening knowledge and skills with electives. Understanding the choices you have, pre-requisites and semesters offered is critical in getting a place into these limited classes.

Comparing architecture styles

Architecture styles differ greatly between Universities in Australia. We wanted to inspire students with an online exhibition of graduating student works

A collection of graduating student's digital works were collected from their graduation studio and were put online to exhibit. We were able to reuse this content to show the results of studying architecture at usyd.

Entry to the University

Getting into University requires years of preparation, there are multiple ways you can get into architecture - like writing a letter to the program director and submitting a portfolio

Project results

Built with mobile first and accessibility in mind. A complete revamp of the architecture faculty experience.
