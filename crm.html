CommBank CRM Digital Transformation

CommSee is one of the biggest in-house CRM ever built by a bank and is strictly proprietary internal tools, therefore I am unable to show you what I worked on. However I can tell you, this product is used in the bank everyday by tens of thousands of CBA staff to transact with customers, financial record-keeping, fraud detection, branch functions, loan decisioning and analytics for retail, business and institutional customers.

Problem space

Hundreds of applications developed internally were never designed for usability and were built and modified with little regards for consistency.

Challenges:
  • Simple transactions grew in complexity due to a mix of old legacy systems and new applications
  • Training staff became costly and complexities posed a compliance and regulatory risk
  • Applications were not used by a single team but shared and used across the group
Strategy:
  • Enterprise modernisation to a mobile and web framework
  • Enterprise Design System was developed, applied and extended by designers working with project scrums
  • Omni-channel services
  • Service design framework was created to better understand the interactions between customers and CBA staff members
  • Frequent observation and testing with CBA staff members on all new projects
Project portfolio

I had a chance to work on a wide array of projects across different business across the bank:

  • Financial crimes and regulatory - Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA) and Common Reporting Standard (CRS)
  • Home Loans - Dynamic Decisioning for home Loans
  • Comprehensive Credit Reporting (CCR) - Interbank credit reporting
  • Settlements for Institutional Banking - Settling of high value loan facilities
